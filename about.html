Learn More About Us

NA SO SNACKS LTD. is an agro-based food manufacturing company that places a premium on quality, care, and consumer preference. Our processes and machinery are the result of extensive research in modern food production and manufacturing. We are a Nigerian-centric business of "Nigerian Pride". Our snack range is a credible brand with the Nigerian colloquialism "Na So", which means "that is it!" Na So is a condensed experience in a beautifully packaged snack variety made possible by the best experts in the field of foods and snacks. Experience the real deal in snacks. Na So quality chops.

Our Mission

  • To be Nigeria's leading producer of food snacks.
  • To provide memorable experiences for all of our product's users.
  • To strengthen our economy by lowering the rate at which Nigerians import goods.
  • In five years, it will be well known locally throughout Nigeria's 36 States and internationally.

Our Vision

  • Our goal is to make our product accessible and affordable to all Nigerians.
  • To strive daily to produce the best snacks on the planet.

Who We Are

OUR GOAL IS YOUR SUCCESS

NA SO SNACKS Ltd is a very large snacks manufacturing company in Nigeria dealing in manufacturing, distribution/Sales, Haulage and Logistics. NA SO SNACKS Ltd was founded in 2019 as RIIM FOODS LTD and fully owned by a team of four directors. In 2021, the company was restructured; the name changed from RIIM FOODS LTD to NA SO SNACKS Ltd and the directors down-sized to three at a stake of 40%, 30% and 30%. The first product manufactured and sold was Roasted burger Peanut and sold massively since 2020, then Sweet Popcorn was released mid-2020 and since then it has played a seminal role in making Sweet Popcorn a household brand in Nigeria. NA SO SNACKS Ltd over the years has grown adding 3 additional brands comprising of Tasty Crunchy which is a cereal (ready-to-eat as breakfast or snacks), Caramel Popcorn and Sweet Popcorn (big value pack). Our technology is an international word class. NA SO SNACKS Ltd anticipates to set its foot beyond the Nigeria borders.
