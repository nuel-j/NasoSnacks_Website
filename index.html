About Us

Learn More About Us

  • To be the number one food snacks producer in Nigeria.
  • To create memorable experiences for all consumers of our products.

NA SO SNACKS LTD. is an agro-based food manufacturing company that places a premium on quality, care, and consumer preference. Our processes and machinery are the result of extensive research in modern food production and manufacturing. We are a Nigerian-centric business of "Nigerian Pride". Our snack range is a credible brand with the Nigerian colloquialism "Na So", which means "that is it!" Na So is a condensed experience in a beautifully packaged snack variety made possible by the best experts in the field of foods and snacks. Experience the real deal in snacks. Na So quality chops.
